HexaDrone Prototype Development

I engineered and led the development of the HexaDrone Prototype for AI-based surveillance, integrating real-time facial recognition to enhance security and military operations. As the team lead, I coordinated all phases, from hardware integration to software development, ensuring seamless performance. Faced with the challenge of slow processing, I optimized the system by switching from FaceNet to LBPH for faster recognition. The drone, built on a ZD850 Hexacopter with a Raspberry Pi 4 and Arducam camera, successfully passed rigorous real-world testing. My leadership in the project presentation to Navy officials led to their interest in acquiring the system for security operations.
